Let's Make Some Tacos!

Description:

Taco is an internationally popular hand-sized food item of Mexican origin. This food item is prepared by combining seasoned meat, some vegetables, and other fillings and it is served inside a folded corn tortilla. It can be enjoyed with dips, like tomato sauce, garlic sauce and many herbal sauces.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef (grounded)
  • 1/2 medium onion (diced)
  • 1/2 medium bell pepper (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 packet oregano seasoning
  • 8-10 corn tortillas
  • cheese (Shredded)
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes (diced)
  • sour cream
  • salsa

Instructions:

  1. In a large non-stick pan, cook grounded beef over medium-high heat until browned, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks.
  2. Next add diced onion and bell pepper to the pan and cook until tender.
  3. Now add garlic, oregano seasoning, and 1/2 cup water to the pan. Stir to combine.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. After that warm tortillas by wrapping them in a damp kitchen towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  6. At last assemble tacos by spooning beef mixture onto a tortilla and top it with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, sour cream, and salsa as per the taste requirement.

Enjoy your delicious tacos!
