The First Frost

It tells the story of two high school classmates' reunion, after long separation, when they accidentally rent the same apartment. It delves into the complexities of love, longing, trauma and healing. During its release, Bai Jingting and Zhang Ruonan were ranked 'Top C-Drama artists in the International Market'.
